Browzifile

Search your files like in a browser

Despite my best attempts to keep my digital life organized, I always hit the same wall: “Where did I put that file?” Even with labeled folders and clean naming systems, search was always a pain. I didn’t want to remember where a file was but I just wanted to describe it and find it, like I do on the web. So I built Browzifile.
Browzifile is a lightweight desktop tool for Windows that lets you search your files by describing them, not just by remembering exact names or folders. It’s inspired by how we use Google: You type what you remember, and the most relevant results appear.
